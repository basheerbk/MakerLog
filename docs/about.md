# About

## Who I Am

I am a **Maker Mentor** at **[iLab](https://ilabindia.org)**, passionate about hands-on learning, prototyping, and helping makers bring their ideas to life.

## What I Do at iLab

- Guide students and makers through hardware and software projects
- Facilitate workshops and skill-building sessions
- Support project planning, prototyping, and iteration
- Build a culture of curiosity and making

## Why I Keep This Journal

This journal helps me:

- Track my growth as a mentor over time
- Document the projects and ideas coming out of iLab
- Reflect on what's working in my mentoring approach
- Share learnings with the broader maker community
