# iLab Maker Journal

Welcome to my personal activity log as a **Maker Mentor** and **STEM Mentor** at **iLab**.

This journal documents my mentoring sessions, the maker projects I've guided, and the learnings that come out of each experience.

---

## What This Journal Covers

- **Mentoring Sessions** — one-on-one and group sessions with makers and students
- **Maker Projects Guided** — projects I've helped design, prototype, or debug
- **Workshops & Activities** — any events, demos, or hands-on activities I facilitated
- **Reflections** — what worked, what didn't, and what's next

---

## Journal Structure

Each log entry follows this format:

```
## Overview
What happened this session.

## Mentees / Participants
Who I worked with.

## Projects Guided
Projects discussed or built.

## Activities
What we did hands-on.

## Highlights
Key wins or breakthroughs.

## Challenges
Blockers or areas to improve.

## Next Steps
Follow-ups or upcoming plans.
```

---

## About the Mentor

I am a Maker Mentor / STEM Mentor at **iLab**, helping students and makers turn ideas into real projects through hands-on guidance and community learning.
